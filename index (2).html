Global Superstore Profitability Analysis
2025
SQL (MySQL) · Excel · 51,290 orders · 147 countries

Discount impact study on a global retail dataset. Used MySQL CTEs, JOINs, and CASE WHEN logic to identify exactly where profit was leaking across regions and product categories, then built an Excel workbook to validate and present findings.

Key finding
